About Meliorist Art

"Meliorism" is a belief that the world can be made better through individual human effort.

Meliorist Art will be actively participating in spooky season and hope to provide memorable experiences for the Hampton Roads/ Norfolk Virginia community.

We may set up shop in a business or you may find us in the middle of a field, or in the woods on a foggy night.

If you are a Psychic/Herbalist/Artist and want to join in on the fun, feel free to reach out.

Available for event booking, both commercial and private

Quick disclaimer: Meliorist Art does not offer tarot readings over the phone or internet.
