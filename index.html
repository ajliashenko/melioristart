Tarot Card Reading? How Does That Work?

Every card has its own individual and predetermined meaning rooted in symbolism. When the cards are shuffled, those symbols and stories of each card are generated at random.
Although tarot is used by some people as a way to channel their psychic ability, or predict the future, it is not a service I offer. I have no special powers or any idea what is going on in your life, I will only be telling you what the individual cards mean as an opportunity for self reflection. You may be wondering, doesn't that mean anyone can do this if they put the time in? The answer is yes.

Quick disclaimer: Meliorist Art does not offer tarot card readings over the phone or internet. Once again, not a psychic.
